AI 101: Fundamentals and Business Impact

24 hours total (6 hours per week) 2,400

What is the Course?

A foundational one-month program designed to introduce beginners to Artificial Intelligence and its business applications. Through hands-on activities and real-world case studies, you'll gain practical experience with AI tools while understanding core concepts in machine learning and neural networks.

Course Objectives

  • Build foundational knowledge in AI concepts and tools
  • Develop practical Python skills for data science
  • Understand machine learning and neural networks
  • Explore real-world business applications of AI
  • Prepare for AI-related career opportunities

What Will You Learn?

  • Core AI/ML concepts and terminology
  • Python for data analysis and visualization
  • Building simple prediction models
  • Implementing neural networks
  • Business case analysis for AI
  • AI toolchain and platforms

What Will the Course Prepare You For?

  • Understanding AI-driven business solutions
  • Entry-level AI/data science roles
  • Implementing basic AI workflows
  • Evaluating AI opportunities in organizations
  • Pursuing advanced AI certifications

Prerequisites

  • Basic computer literacy
  • High school math understanding
  • No prior coding experience required
